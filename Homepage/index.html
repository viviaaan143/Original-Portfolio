BusyBus

BusyBus is an intuitive application that clearly shows the location and time of bus lines and tracks the user's location. It eliminates the guessing game of "when will the bus arrive?", "is this the correct bus for me?" and users will have confidence in knowing that they are on the right track to their destination in a timely manner.
