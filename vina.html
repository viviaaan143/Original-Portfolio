Visual Design

After meeting with my client, I had learned her vision for her nail shop. Her enthusiasm for organic, healthy living heavily influenced her use of quality products for her salon. Beyond painting nails, she wanted her clients to be comfortable, relaxed, and refreshed. This helped me understand where to lead the branding.



After many sketches, I created a few of her favorite designs on Figma to help her visualize it more.



This is the chosen logo. It is elegant, simplistic, and natural-looking. The branches have a fluid flow to it, which is really lovely.

Low-Fi to Mockups



Because I wanted to entertain the idea of what the website would look like, I went ahead and designed it.

Conclusion

I'm really glad I decided to take the project further. This project helped me think from a business perspective. For example, this salon has 3 package options so it is helpful to indicate which is most popular so website visitors will see that one option first instead of being overwhelmed. Pictures are also very important to websites, so it's great to have pictures that are cohesive and further develop the branding. Thank you, Unsplash, for providing these images!
